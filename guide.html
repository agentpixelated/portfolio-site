Portfolio — Design Guide

Built following the $10K Site Playbook methodology.

Concept

A portfolio for an AI Systems Architect & Quantitative Analyst. The design reflects precision, warmth, and depth.

Signature Technique

Particle choreography — warm gold particles drifting on a canvas, connected by faint lines, responding to cursor proximity. Particles within 200px of the cursor are pushed away gently and brighten.

Palette — "Warm Noir"

  • #0a0908 — Deep warm black (never pure #000)
  • #f5f1e8 — Warm off-white (never pure #FFF)
  • #c4a669 — Gold accent
  • #8c6b4f — Muted bronze
  • #1a1815 — Charcoal

Type System

  • Display: Playfair Display (700–900 weight, clamp up to 10rem)
  • Body: Inter (300–600 weight)
  • Mono: JetBrains Mono (labels, coordinates, tags)

Structure Device

Section labels use geographic coordinates (06°12'S 106°49'E — Jakarta) and chronological markers.
